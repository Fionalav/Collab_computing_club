About Columbia ComputRs

The group was founded by Julia Wrobel and Ariel Chernofsky under faculty advisor Christine Mauro in the Department of Biostatistics at Columbia University.

We meet monthly in NYC to learn about statistical concepts related to programming and computing. See the schedule for a list of upcoming meetings. Other contributing members to the blog and tutorial content are Annie Lee, Jiaming (Jerry) Huang, Jiehui Xu, and Qingling Li.
