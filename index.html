Welcome to the the Columbia Biostatistics Computing Club!

The group is run by Julia Wrobel and Ariel Chernofsky under faculty advisor Christine Mauro in the Department of Biostatistics at Columbia University.

We host causal monthly seminars focusing on computational topics often encountered in Biostatistics research. The tutorials provide hands-on learning at a variety of skill levels, with some focused on bringing new students up to speed and others addressing more advanced problems. Our overarching goal is to impart good programming practices that encourage collaborative reproducible research! See the schedule for a list of upcoming meetings.

Find R markdown files for past topics under resources tab.
